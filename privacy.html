CarLink Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

CarLink is a personal Android Automotive OS application. This policy describes how the app handles data on your device.

Data collected

CarLink does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All processing happens entirely on your device.

Microphone

CarLink requests the RECORD_AUDIO permission solely to support voice commands through the Android Automotive OS voice input system. Audio is processed locally by the OS voice stack and is never recorded, stored, or sent anywhere by this app.

Other permissions

  • USB — used to communicate with the CarLink USB adapter.
  • Location — used to forward the vehicle's GPS position to the connected phone for navigation overlays.
  • Media / Notifications — used to surface playback controls in the system UI and on the instrument cluster.

None of these permissions are used to collect or transmit data.

Third-party services

CarLink does not integrate any analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting SDKs.

Contact

For questions, contact the developer through Google Play.
